Token distribution

The more Ether everyone contributes, the more tokens you get.

The base token distribution formula is very simple: for every 1 ETH you contribute, you'll receive 1000 DDT, and if you contribute at least 0.01 ETH, you'll also receive an additional 1 DDT for every Ether that's been contributed in total. Like this:

Transaction Ether sent Total Ether sent DDT received
#1 1.000  ETH 1.000  ETH 1001.000  DDT
#2 3.000  ETH 4.000  ETH 3004.000  DDT
#3 0.005  ETH 4.005  ETH 5.000  DDT
#4 0.500  ETH 4.505  ETH 504.505  DDT

Bonus tokens!

Every block has a 1 in 256 chance of issuing bonus tokens.

When purchasing DADDY TOKEN, the smart contract will take a hash of the current block's coinbase, number, and timestamp. If the first byte of the hash is 0x00, the block becomes a bonus block. During a bonus block, the smart contract counts the number of bits set in the second byte of the hash and uses that number as a bonus multiplier.

Every DADDY purchase of at least 0.01 ETH worth during the bonus block will receive, in addition to the base distribution, an additional 100 DDT per Ether multiplied by the bonus multiplier. That means that contributors have a chance of receiving a total of nine times the number of tokens they expected!
